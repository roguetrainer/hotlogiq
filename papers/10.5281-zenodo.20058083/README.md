# Hardy's Paradox and the Fano Associator: A Geometric Diagnosis of Quantum Contextuality

**Paper:** 269 | **Portfolio F** — Quantum Foundations

**DOI:** [10.5281/zenodo.20058083](https://zenodo.org/records/20058083)

## Abstract

The bipartite Hardy paradox is given a geometric diagnosis: the octonion associator ‖A‖=0 on Fano-line triples is the algebraic engine enabling P(Z+,Z+)>0. Quantum contextuality is identified with Fano-line membership.
